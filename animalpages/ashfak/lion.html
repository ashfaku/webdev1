Lion

Lions (Panthera leo) are large, powerful carnivores known as the "kings of the jungle," though they primarily inhabit grasslands and savannas in Africa and a small region in India. Unlike most big cats, lions are highly social and live in groups called prides, which typically consist of related females, their cubs, and a few dominant males. Male lions are recognized by their thick manes, which serve as a display of strength and help protect them during fights.
